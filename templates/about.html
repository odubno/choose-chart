Inspiration

This is my community project for my Data Visualization class at Columbia.
In order to help me study what charts are best for what type of data,
I chose to make an app that would explain it.

This app guides the user through different types

of data and charts that are best to explore findings with.


You may notice no mention of piecharts.

That's because what can be communicated with a pie chart, bar graphs and histograms could communicate it better.

There are mixed emotions on pie charts, that I will not get into. Use them at your own risk. I'm avoiding them for now.
